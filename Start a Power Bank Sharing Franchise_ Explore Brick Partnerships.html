Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any startup costs?
There is no startup cost, only the cost of purchasing stations and the monthly station fee of 5 per station. Market Operators also pay a yearly, or monthly, partner fee for support and beneficial terms.
What sets Brick apart from other power bank sharing companies?
What sets Brick apart from other powerbank sharing companies is that we offer our Network and Market Operator partners the opportunity to license our platform to use our brand and service and upon signing everything is ready to go. We are a power bank network brand! Other powerbank sharing companies have a label where they employ and expand (white label), but we are offering this as a franchise where you have our brand, and platform, and we help with everything technical.
What types of opportunities and partnerships are there with Brick?
We have two different types of partnerships that we offer at Brick, the Network partnership and the Market Operator partnership. Network partner: Get access to the network, place your stations, and collect revenue. As a NP you strive to build a passive income stream, and the know-how of the entire Brick Network serves to your purpose. Market Operator: Claim exclusivity in the market and make your network the only real option for power bank sharing - as a MO you set up to achieve market saturation, and you get all the support necessary to do so.
